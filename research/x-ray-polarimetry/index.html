X-ray Polarimetry

Polarization measurements used to constrain emission geometry and magnetized accretion flows.

Polarimetry adds a geometric diagnostic that complements timing and spectroscopy. The material collected here includes work on X-ray polarization detections and talks focused on using polarization data to test pulsar geometry constraints.

Focus on this site

  • Polarization detections in X-ray pulsars
