High-Mass X-ray Binaries

Observational studies of compact objects interacting with massive stellar companions.

Several publications on this site address high-mass X-ray binaries, including source characterization, broadband properties, and the identification of optical companions. Grouping them here creates a clear landing page for readers interested in binary systems rather than a single source.

Focus on this site

  • Source characterization across multiple observatories
  • Optical counterpart identification and system context
  • Persistent and transient binary systems
