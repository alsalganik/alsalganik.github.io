X-ray Timing

Variability, quasi-periodic oscillations, and timing transitions in accreting neutron-star systems.

Timing analysis is one of the strongest recurring themes across the publication and talk record. These pages group together work on pulse evolution, oscillatory behavior, and transitions that reveal how the accretion flow changes with time.
