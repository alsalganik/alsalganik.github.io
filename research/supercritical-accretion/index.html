Supercritical Accretion

Transitions into high-accretion regimes and the observational signatures of supercritical X-ray pulsars.

Supercritical accretion appears repeatedly in the publication list, observing programs, and press links. This topic page collects the source-specific results that point to luminosity-state transitions and changes in the structure of the accretion flow.
